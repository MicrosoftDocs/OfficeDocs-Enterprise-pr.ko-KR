You might need an isolated site for the following:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="a60a2-113">조직 또는 종속 되는에서 수행 하는 법적 작업에 대 한 리소스입니다.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a60a2-113">The resources for a legal action taken by your organization or that to which it is being subjected.</span></span>
